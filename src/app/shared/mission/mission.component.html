Grow & Tellâ€™s mission is to provide Speech-Language Pathology services, including the prevention, screening, assessment, treatment, and counseling/collaboration of communication disorders to clients in infancy through adolescents. Grow & Tell aims to deliver individualized, evidenced based and developmentally supported services to clients, while also providing support to their families. We believe familial support and involvement through the therapy process is essential for carryover and generalization of skills from the therapy setting to everyday routines and activities. Grow & Tell involves the parents/family in the therapy process by teaching them how to implement strategies that target individualized goals. Grow & Tell is located within Carlisle Pediatric Associates in Carlisle, PA. Therapy services are delivered through a play based approach (infants & toddlers), and/or a traditional drill-based approach (school age & adolescents), depending on the age and needs of the client.
